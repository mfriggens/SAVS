Score Interpretation

Positive scores (+) indicate vulnerability - the species may be negatively affected by climate change in this category.

Negative scores (-) indicate resilience - the species may benefit from or be less affected by climate change in this category.

Scores near zero suggest neutral effects or mixed impacts.

Uncertainty scores reflect the quality and availability of information. High uncertainty paired with neutral scores may indicate lack of data rather than true neutral effects.
